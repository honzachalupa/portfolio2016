About me

Hello there, my name is Honza. I’m from Prague, Czech Republic.

I grew up in front of computer screen and in these days was born my passion for computers and everything about it. It started with playing video-games followed by learning about vector graphics, Flash animations, bitmap graphics and photography, creating of basics websites back in 2008 and even mobile and desktop apps later after that.

And now I’m here, getting the most of I learned since I was 5-year-old playing Transport Tycoon on dad’s old computer together and trying to create something new, something unordinary.
